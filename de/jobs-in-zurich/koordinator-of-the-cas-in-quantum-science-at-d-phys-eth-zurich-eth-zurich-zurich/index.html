Bewerbungsprozess

  • The Certificate of Advanced Studies (CAS) in Quantum Science at ETH Zurich is designed to provide digital technology experts, engineers, and professionals in banking or government with a strong foundation in quantum science and its emerging applications.
  • Bringing together expertise from across D-PHYS and the ETH Quantum Center, the programme combines high-level academic teaching with insights into foundations and current developments in quantum technologies.
  • The CAS Coordinator will play a central role in the development, launch, and implementation of the programme and will serve as the key interface between the CAS directors, participants, lecturers, the ETH School for Continuing Education (SCE), and other internal and external partners. Job description
  • As the Coordinator of CAS in Quantum Science, you will oversee and contribute to the development, launch, and implementation of the CAS in Quantum Science.
  • You will work closely with the CAS directors, D-PHYS professors and study administration, ITP administration and act as the central point of contact for the ETH SCE, as well as with other relevant units at ETH Zurich, including the ETH Quantum Center.
