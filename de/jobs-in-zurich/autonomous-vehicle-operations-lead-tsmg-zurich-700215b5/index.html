  • Lead the daily operations of autonomous vehicle and robotaxi fleets across one or multiple depot locations.

  • Monitor fleet utilization, vehicle availability, charging schedules, and operational readiness.
  • Coordinate daily vehicle dispatch and return activities to maximize fleet efficiency.
  • Minimize vehicle downtime through effective operational planning and coordination.
  • Oversee all activities within the robotaxi depot, ensuring safe, efficient, and organized operations.
