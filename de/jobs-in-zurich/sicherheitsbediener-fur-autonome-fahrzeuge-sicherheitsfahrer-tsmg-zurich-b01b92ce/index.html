Hauptaufgaben

  • Safely operate and monitor autonomous vehicles during testing, validation, and commercial robotaxi fleet operations.
  • Continuously supervise the performance of the self-driving system while maintaining full awareness of surrounding traffic conditions.
  • Take immediate manual control of the vehicle whenever required by operational procedures or safety protocols.
  • Complete assigned routes while ensuring compliance with local traffic regulations and company operating procedures.
  • Support the successful deployment of autonomous ride-hailing services by maintaining safe and consistent vehicle operation.
  • Conduct pre-trip and post-trip inspections to ensure each autonomous vehicle is deployment-ready.
  • Verify vehicle cleanliness and inspect cameras, sensors, mirrors, lighting, and other critical systems before each shift.
  • Report any damage, abnormal vehicle behaviour, or maintenance concerns to depot and technical teams.
  • Support overall fleet readiness by ensuring vehicles remain available for daily operations.
  • Record disengagements, observations, and operational events using company reporting tools.

  • Support continuous improvement initiatives aimed at increasing the safety and efficiency of autonomous fleet operations.
  • You do not need previous experience working with autonomous vehicles - our comprehensive training program will prepare you to safely support the testing, deployment, and commercial operation of self-driving vehicles.
