Rollenüberblick

Application Engineer II As an Application Engineer II in our Product Area, you will contribute to the development of a modern lakehouse architecture build on Azure Datalake using Python to implement business services. You will implement business functionality that enables our customers to run their accounting processes and generate data to meet their reporting needs. You will design, develop, automate, and support backend applications that combine data elements from multiple domains and systems. You will leverage innovative technologies and frameworks to enable scalable and efficient processing. You will collaborate with other engineers, analysts, product owners and stakeholders to deliver value-added solutions that meet the business needs and expectations.

We value clean code, automation, continuous improvement, knowledge sharing and engineering excellence. We actively leverage AI-assisted development tools such as GitHub Copilot to improve engineering productivity and quality. We are living DevOps. We own the implementation of the business functionality and operate our workload in production. We are looking for an ambitious application engineer, who will help to build and deploy business services to production environments.
